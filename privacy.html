Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025 · Effective: June 1, 2025

🔒 Your privacy matters. Axexvx.link collects only what's necessary to operate our service. We never sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

1. Who We Are

Axexvx.link is a URL shortening and link intelligence platform operated by Axexvx Technologies. For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@axexvx.link.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and hashed password at registration.
  • Billing Data: Payment info processed securely via Stripe. We never store card numbers.
  • Link Data: URLs you shorten, custom aliases, and link settings.
  • Click Analytics: IP addresses (anonymized after 24h), browser, OS, country, referrer, and click timestamps.
  • SMS Data: Phone numbers uploaded for campaigns, message content, and delivery status.
  • Usage Data: API calls, feature usage, session duration, and interaction logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Axexvx.link services
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To provide click analytics and campaign reports
  • To detect fraud, abuse, malware, and phishing links
  • To send transactional emails (account, billing, security alerts)
  • To send marketing emails (opt-out available at any time)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to deliver our services
  • Legitimate interests: Security, fraud prevention, analytics
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional cookies
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws

5. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions and security. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymized analytics to understand platform usage.
  • Preference cookies: To remember your dashboard settings and language.

6. Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted third-party service providers: Stripe (payments), Twilio (SMS), Cloudflare (CDN/security), AWS (infrastructure), and Postmark (email). We never sell, rent, or trade your data.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Until deletion + 30 days for recovery
  • Click analytics: IP anonymized after 24h; aggregate data retained indefinitely
  • SMS recipient lists: Deleted 90 days after campaign completion
  • Billing records: 7 years for legal compliance

8. Security

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
  • Malware and phishing detection on every submitted link
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) available for all accounts

9. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any right, email privacy@axexvx.link. We respond within 30 days.

10. International Transfers

We operate globally. Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international transfers from the EEA.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data was collected, we delete it promptly.

12. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We notify you of significant changes via email at least 14 days before they take effect.
